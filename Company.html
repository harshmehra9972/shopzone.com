About New Jaisa

NewJaisa is thrilled to be a driving force in the growth of the refurbished electronics market in India. . Our mission is to prove that technology and sustainability can coexist, and our journey is only just beginning. We believe in the democratization of technology, making it accessible and affordable for all.

We firmly believe there’s no need to pay a premium for quality or for a sustainable choice. Our process creates true circularity when we offer customers to buy refurbished and extend their life cycle by refurbishing and reintroducing them back to the market with a warranty. By keeping existing laptops longer in use we reduce e-waste and give people an opportunity to create a positive environmental impact by purchasing refurbished instead of new.

NewJaisa drove consumer awareness and trust in the sector by standardizing quality, championing the role of sustainability, and making buying refurbished electronics as common as buying a used car.” By empowering corporate, individuals and institutions to make conscious choices, we're not only keeping them connected, but also building a greener tomorrow, one device at a time."

Our story

Not so long ago in 2020, the thought of purchasing a used laptop was a foreign concept to many in India. But at NewJaisa, we saw an opportunity to change that. Our goal was to establish a reputable and reliable online destination where individuals could find high-quality, refurbished laptops/desktop and results are for everyone to see. Refurbished laptops and desktops today are among the best sellers in laptop/desktop categories and NewJaisa is the largest seller of refurbished laptops/desktops in India and have sold more than 50000+ devices.
